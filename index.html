The originally determinate nature of individuality, its immediate essence, is not yet posited as active, and as such is called special capacity, talent, character, and so on. This peculiar tinge of spirit is to be looked on as the sole content of the end itself and as the sole reality. If we thought of consciousness as going beyond that, and as wanting to give reality to a different content, then we should be thinking of it as a nothing working towards nothing.
